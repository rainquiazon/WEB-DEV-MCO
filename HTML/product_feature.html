About Flins

Full Name: Kyryll Chudomirovich Flins

Title: Shadowy Lights, Stranger Wights

Region / Affiliation: Nod-Krai, Lightkeepers

Vision (Element): Electro

Weapon Type: Polearm

Rarity: ★★★★★ (5-Star)

Release Date: September 30, 2025 (Version 6.0 Phase 2)

Features & Abilities

  • Elemental Skill: Infuses his polearm with Electro, causing Normal and Charged Attacks to deal Electro damage.
  • Elemental Burst: Unleashes a powerful AoE Electro attack. Some of its damage is “Lunar-Charged.”
  • Lore: A Lightkeeper who watches over a lighthouse and graveyard in Nod-Krai. Known for his refined manners and tragic past.
  • Constellation: Laterna Vigilis
