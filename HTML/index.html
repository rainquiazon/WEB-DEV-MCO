About Me

Hey, I’m Jose Urbanito S. Ricafrente III, but most people just call me Thirdy.
I’m not the kind of person who craves attention or plays by everyone else’s rules.
I keep things simple — I value peace, quiet, and solitude. A dark room, a hot cup of coffee, and silence? That’s my idea of comfort.
I’m serious by nature, but not cold. I just believe some things are better left unspoken — after all, “speak less to make your words effective.”
When I’m not working or thinking too much, you’ll find me grinding in competitive games like Valorant.
I’m drawn to intense challenges, which is probably why I love Souls games that make your brain melt and your reflexes sharp.

Skills & Hobbies

Competitive FPS Gamer

Competitive games like Valorant not only sharpen my reflexes and strategic thinking but also enhance my teamwork, communication skills, and ability to stay calm under pressure. These games challenge me to adapt quickly, analyze opponents' strategies, and make split-second decisions, which translate well into real-world problem-solving and focus.

Souls Game Enthusiast

I enjoy playing Souls games, especially Elden Ring, for their intense and rewarding challenges, intricate world-building, and deep lore. These games push me to develop patience, precision, and strategic thinking, requiring me to learn from mistakes and constantly adapt my approach. The immersive environments and complex narratives keep me fully engaged, offering a unique blend of exploration, combat, and problem-solving that fuels both my creativity and critical thinking.

Music Lover

I love listening to music with calm beats and soothing lullaby vibes, especially songs by Arthur Nery. His smooth vocals and mellow melodies create the perfect atmosphere for relaxation and reflection, helping me unwind after a long day. Music like this inspires my creativity and provides a peaceful backdrop for focus, whether I’m working, studying, or simply taking a moment to recharge.
